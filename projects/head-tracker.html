Overview

Custom-built head tracking module for an FPV RC car, fully 3D printed and programmed using Arduino. The module translates head movements into camera orientation commands for immersive remote driving experiences.

Design and Fabrication

The enclosure, mount, and mechanical parts were designed in CAD and 3D printed for precise fit. Electronics integration included an MPU6050 IMU and signal interfacing to override potentiometer channels for control of the car’s servo mechanisms.

Challenges and Solutions

A key challenge was minimizing sensor drift and ensuring smooth tracking. This was overcome using filtered PWM signals, calibration routines, and software corrections to maintain accurate alignment between the head motion and the FPV camera orientation.
